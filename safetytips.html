Essential Safety Tips for Women & Children

1. Stay Aware of Your Surroundings

Avoid distractions like excessive phone use in public places. Be alert to any suspicious activity around you.

2. Emergency Contact Setup

Always keep emergency contacts saved in your phone and enable quick dial shortcuts for them.

3. Self-Defense Techniques

Learn basic self-defense moves such as blocking, striking, and escaping grips. Taking self-defense classes can be beneficial.

5. Trust Your Instincts

If you feel uncomfortable in any situation, trust your gut and find a safe way to remove yourself from that scenario.

6. Public Transport Safety

Always share your ride details with someone you trust when traveling alone. Prefer well-lit and populated areas when waiting for transport.

7. Avoid Oversharing on Social Media

Do not share your live location or personal details publicly. Criminals can use this information against you.

8. Carry a Personal Safety Device

Keep a pepper spray, whistle, or safety alarm in your bag for emergency situations.

9. Safe Online Interactions

Do not share personal information with strangers online. Be cautious when meeting someone from the internet.

10. Know the Emergency Numbers

Memorize important emergency numbers and store them in your phone.
